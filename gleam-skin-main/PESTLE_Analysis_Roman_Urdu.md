# PESTLE Analysis - Survonica (Roman Urdu)

## 1. Political (Siyasi)
*   **Data Sovereignty:** Pakistan mein data ki hifazat ke qanoon sakht ho rahe hain.
*   **Government Support:** 'Digital Pakistan' policy aur Ignite/NIC jaise idaray startups ko support kar rahe hain.

## 2. Economic (Maashi)
*   **Recession Trend:** Jab maishat kharab hoti hai toh log saste software dhundte hain, jo hamare liye faida mand hai.
*   **Currency Arbitrage:** Hum karcher PKR (Rupee) mein kar rahe hain lekin revenue USD (Dollar) mein kama sakte hain.

## 3. Social (Samaji)
*   **Mobile-First Behavior:** Gen Z aur Millennials mobile par surveys pasand karte hain.
*   **Privacy Consciousness:** Aaj kal ke log apni privacy ke baaray mein bohot sensitive hain.

## 4. Technological (Fanni)
*   **Generative AI:** Llama 3 aur GPT-4 ki wajah se naye raste khul gaye hain.
*   **Cloud Infrastructure:** AWS aur doosray cloud platforms ki wajah se software ko scale karna asan ho gaya hai.

## 5. Legal (Qanooni)
*   **AI Copyright:** AI se banaye gaye content ke copyright qanoon abhi ban rahe hain.
*   **GDPR Compliance:** International market mein kaam karne ke liye GDPR jaise sakht rules follow karne parenge.

## 6. Environmental (Maholiyati)
*   **Paperless Operations:** Digital surveys ki wajah se kaghaz ka istemal kam hota hai jo mahol ke liye behtar hai.
*   **Green Cloud:** Hum koshish karenge ke carbon-neutral cloud servers istemal karain.
