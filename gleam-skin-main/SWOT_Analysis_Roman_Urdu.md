# SWOT Analysis - Survonica (Roman Urdu)

## Strengths (Taqat)
1. **AI-Native Architecture:** Survonica shuru se hi AI ke liye bani hai, yeh koi purana tool nahi jis mein AI baad mein dala gaya ho.
2. **Kam Kharcha (Low Cost):** Serverless architecture ki wajah se hamare operational costs bohot kam hain.
3. **Local Focus:** Hum ne South Asia aur academic market ko zehan mein rakh kar feature banaye hain.
4. **Asan User Interface (UX):** Survonica ka design bohot modern aur use karne mein asan hai.

## Weaknesses (Kamzoriyan)
1. **Naya Brand:** Market mein naya hone ki wajah se logo ka trust jeetne mein waqt lagega.
2. **Choti Team:** Abhi hamare resources aur team choti hai.
3. **3rd Party Nirbhar (Dependency):** Hum AI models ke liye bahar ki companies (APIs) par depend karte hain.

## Opportunities (Maukay)
1. **Prosumer Market:** Woh log jo Google Forms se agay barhna chahte hain lekin SurveyMonkey jitna kharcha nahi kar sakte.
2. **SaaS Export:** Hum apna software Pakistan se bahar MENA region (Middle East) mein export kar sakte hain.
3. **Integrations:** Survonica ko Slack, Notion aur doosray tools ke sath jorna asan hai.

## Threats (Khatray)
1. **Big Tech Companies:** Google ya Microsoft agar is tarah ka AI tool le ayain.
2. **Regulatory Tabdeeli:** Data privacy aur AI ke naye qanoon mushkil paida kar sakte hain.
3. **Clone Competitors:** Doosray log hamare idea ko jaldi copy kar sakte hain.
