# Business Plan - Survonica (Roman Urdu)

## 1. Executive Summary (Khulasa)
Survonica aik AI-powered platform hai jo surveys banane aur data analyze karne ka tareeqa badal raha hai. Hamara maqsad har student aur entrepreneur ko expert-level research tools dena hai.

## 2. Market Problem (Masla kya hai?)
Moajooda tools ya toh bohot mehnge hain (SurveyMonkey) ya phir un mein AI nahi hai (Google Forms). Logon ko survey design karne mein mushkil hoti hai aur ghalat data milne ka khatra hota hai.

## 3. Solution (Hamara Hal)
Hum ne aik aisa tool banaya hai jo:
*   Sirf bolne ya likhne se survey bana deta hai.
*   Fake responses ko khud reject kar deta hai.
*   Data ko asan dashboards mein dikhata hai.

## 4. Market Strategy (Hum market mein kaise jayenge?)
*   **Pehla Saal:** Pakistan ki badi universities (NUST, LUMS, Namal) mein student ambassadors banayenge.
*   **Doosra Saal:** Middle East aur Europe ki markets ko target karenge.
*   **Teesra Saal:** Professional HR aur Research companies ko onboarding karenge.

## 5. Financial Goals (Maashi Ahdaf)
*   **Pehla Saal:** 20 paying customers (Revenue: 10 Lakh PKR).
*   **Teesra Saal:** 2,000 paying customers (Revenue: 10 Crore PKR).
*   **Investment:** Humain 18 mahino ke liye 50 Lakh PKR ki investment chahiye.
