# Porter's Five Forces Analysis - Survonica (Roman Urdu)

## 1. Threat of New Entrants (Naye Logon ka Khatra) - High
Form banane wala software banana mushkil nahi hai, is liye naye log asani se aa sakte hain. 
*   **Defense:** Hamari asli taqat AI Intelligence aur data ki safai (Audit Engine) hai jo har koi asani se nahi bana sakta.

## 2. Bargaining Power of Buyers (Khareedaron ki Taqat) - Moderate
Individual users asani se doosray tool par ja sakte hain, lekin badi companies ke liye apna data move karna mushkil hota hai.
*   **Defense:** Hum unique features aur behtar qeemat de kar logon ko sath jore rakhenge.

## 3. Threat of Substitutes (Mutabadil ka Khatra) - Moderate
Log surveys ke bajaye social media polls ya phone calls bhi kar sakte hain.
*   **Defense:** Social media polls mein woh technical analytics nahi milti jo Survonica deta hai.

## 4. Bargaining Power of Suppliers (Providers ki Taqat) - Low to Moderate
Hum AWS ya Hugging Face jaise providers use karte hain. Agar ek provider prices badhata hai toh hum doosray par ja sakte hain.
*   **Defense:** Multi-cloud strategy aur open-source models ka istemal.

## 5. Competitive Rivalry (Muqabla) - High
SurveyMonkey aur Typeform jaise bade naam pehle se mojud hain.
*   **Defense:** Survonica un se sasta hai aur shuru se hi AI par mabni hai (AI-Native).
