# Business Model - Survonica (Roman Urdu)

## 1. Value Proposition (Hum kya khas de rahe hain?)
*   **AI Survey Generation:** Ghanton ka kaam minton mein.
*   **Real-time Analytics:** Data ko foran charts aur insights mein tabdeel karna.
*   **Respondent Screening:** Ghalat ya fake responses ko khud-ba-khud rokna.
*   **Sasta aur Behtar:** Mehnge tools ke muqablay mein behtar features kam qeemat mein.

## 2. Customer Segments (Hamare khareedar kaun hain?)
*   **HR Managers:** Employees ki raye janne ke liye.
*   **University Students:** Apni research aur thesis ke liye.
*   **Startup Founders:** Customer feedback lene ke liye.

## 3. Revenue Model (Hum paise kaise kamayenge?)
Hum "Freemium" model use karte hain:
*   **Free Plan:** $0 (Bunyaadi features ke sath).
*   **Pro Plan:** $15/month (Unlimited surveys aur advanced analytics).
*   **Team Plan:** $40/month (Team collaboration ke liye).
*   **Enterprise Plan:** $99/month (Badi companies ke liye).

## 4. Channels (Hum logon tak kaise pohanchte hain?)
*   **Website:** Hamari apni site jahan se log sign up karte hain.
*   **Social Media:** LinkedIn aur Twitter par marketing.
*   **Universities:** Student ambassadors ke zariye.

## 5. Key Resources (Hamare paas kya hona chahiye?)
*   **AI Technology:** Humare apne trained models.
*   **Cloud Infrastructure:** Data store karne ke liye servers.
*   **Team:** Developers aur sales ka amla.
