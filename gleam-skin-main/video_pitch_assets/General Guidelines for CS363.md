## Project Details
You must clearly describe your **Final Year Project technology** and explain the real-world problem it solves. Justify why this problem matters, identify the target users, state the solution type (product, service, or digital platform), and explain the motivation.

### Project Tasks

#### 1. Problem Statement
Define the real-world problem clearly. It should be specific, relevant, and supported by logic or evidence. Explain who faces it, why it matters, current solutions, and existing gaps.

#### 2. Executive Summary
A concise summary of the entire business idea. Explain the problem, solution, target market, opportunity, revenue generation, and high-level financial/growth outcomes.

#### 3. Company Description
Describe the proposed company, including mission, long-term vision, product/service offering, and current development status of the FYP. State the assumed legal structure and ownership.

#### 4. Industry Analysis
*   **Overview:** Industry size, growth rate, structure, and key trends.
*   **PESTLE Framework:** Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, and Environmental factors.
*   **Porter’s Five Forces:** Threat of new entrants, bargaining power of customers/suppliers, threat of substitutes, and competitive rivalry.
*   **SWOT Analysis:** Internal strengths/weaknesses and external opportunities/threats.

#### 5. Market Analysis
Define the target market, customer needs, pain points, preferences, and buying behavior. Identify direct/indirect competitors and current customer solutions.

#### 6. Value Proposition and Marketing Strategy
Articulate the unique benefits of your solution. Describe the marketing strategy: positioning, pricing, promotion, and distribution/access.

#### 7. Business Model and Revenue Model
Explain how value is created, delivered, and captured. Identify revenue streams, major cost components, pricing logic, and scalability/sustainability.

#### 8. Operations Plan
*   **Technical:** Architecture, software stack, platforms, hosting/cloud infrastructure, security, and data management.
*   **Front Stage:** User interaction and customer support.
*   **Back Stage:** Development workflows, testing, updates, monitoring, and maintenance.

#### 9. Product or Service Design and Development
Outline the current development stage and remaining tasks. Discuss technical challenges, risks, testing strategies, validation methods, and intellectual property.

#### 10. Management Team and Company Structure
Identify the founding team, roles, and responsibilities. Highlight technical expertise and complementary skills. Identify skill gaps and future hiring/advisory needs.

#### 11. Partnership Network
Identify key external partners (cloud providers, API providers, vendors, etc.) and explain their role in operations or scalability.

#### 12. Financial Projections
Prepare realistic projections: sources/uses of funds, assumptions, income statements, cash flow statements, and balance sheets.

#### 13. Risk Analysis
Identify technical, market, operational, and financial risks. Propose realistic mitigation strategies.

#### 14. Milestones and Execution Plan
Define short-term and long-term milestones with a realistic timeline linked to measurable outcomes.

#### 15. Pitch Presentation Requirement (Presentation + Viva)
Prepare a professional pitch communicating the problem, solution, market, technology, competition, business model, and financial outlook suitable for investors or academic panels.
