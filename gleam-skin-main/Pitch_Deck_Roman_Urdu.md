# Survonica Pitch - Roman Urdu

## 1. The Hook (Dilchasp Shuruat)
"Kya aap ko pata hai ke har saal companies aur researchers ke hazaron ghantay sirf aik blank screen ko dekhne mein guzhar jatay hain? Sirf is liye ke unhein samajh nahi aata ke sahi sawal kya poochne hain. Aur jab woh mushkil se survey bana letay hain, toh 70% log boring design ki wajah se usay beech mein hi chor detay hain. Survonica is purane aur thakay huye tareeqay ko hamesha ke liye badalne aaya hai."

## 2. The Problem (Asli Masla)
Aaj ke dor mein data sab se bara hathyar hai, lekin usay jama karna aik azab ban chuka hai:
*   **Design Paralysis:** Google Forms sirf aik khali kaghaz deta hai, woh aap ki madad nahi karta ke behtar survey kaise banaya jaye.
*   **Survey Fatigue:** Log purane aur lambay forms se tang aa chukay hain.
*   **Fake Data:** Bots aur ghalat jawab dene walay poori research ka bera gharak kar detay hain.

## 3. The Solution (Hamara Hal)
Survonica aik **AI-Native Intelligence Tool** hai. Yeh sirf aik form builder nahi, balkay aap ka "Research Assistant" hai.
*   **Conversational AI:** Aap ko sawal likhne ki zarurat nahi. Sirf AI ko batayein ke aap kya janna chahte hain, aur woh minton mein aik scientific survey tayyar kar dega.
*   **Voice-First:** Aap apni raye bol kar bhi record kar saktay hain, jo feedback ko mazeed asan bana deta hai.
*   **Audit Engine:** Hamara system har response ko check karta hai. Agar koi bot hai ya koi ghalat jawab de raha hai, toh woh foran pakra jata hai.

## 4. Value Proposition (Hum kyun behtar hain?)
*   **Economy of Time:** 4 ghantay ka kaam ab sirf 15 minutes mein.
*   **Unbiased Data:** AI ki madad se hum insani galtiyon ko khatam karte hain taake aap ko 100% sahi results milein.
*   **Affordable Excellence:** SurveyMonkey jaisa mehnga software ab har student aur chotay startup ki pohanch mein hai.

## 5. Vision (Mustaqbil)
Humara maqsad "Feedback Friction" ko khatam karna hai. Hum chahte hain ke har faisla data ki bunyaad par ho, aur data jama karna aik bojh nahi balkay aik dilchasp tajurba ban jaye.
